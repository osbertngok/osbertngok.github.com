Osbert Ngok

is a full-stack developer at Messer Financial Software focusing on front-end development. He specialized in canvas-based rich feature spreadsheet JavaScript development and continuous integration. Previously he worked for Deloitte Consulting and UBS.

In his personal life he is also interested in productivity and long-term goal management. Since 2007 he has written a dozen of Personal Information Management (PIM) systems to assist users to review and improve their life, so they can get closer to who they want themselves to be.

He is an amateur pianist and guitarist. In addition to do actual composition by himself, he also delegates the job to programs. In 2011 he partnered with Yu Yue to develop an algorithm to compose fugues.
