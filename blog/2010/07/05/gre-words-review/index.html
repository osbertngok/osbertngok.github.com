FAQ:

Q1 How to use it?

A1

  1. Enter “greXXX”(XXX represents 1-54, quotation marks not included) after “Set:” then hit “Begin!” Button. use “left click” or “J” key to indicate you know the word. Use “right click” or “K” key to indicate you don’t. Every test will pick 30 words randomly from a word list extracted from Red Bible 2007 Edition. All “incorrect” words will be stored in the textarea at the bottom of the page.
  2. After you finish the test, click “Filtered Test” to test the words you previously selected “I don’t know it”. You may do filtered test however times you want.
  3. You can save the “wrong word list” into any one of those slots provided by clicking “save X” Buttons. Those slots will be emptied every time you close the page (or browser).

Q2 What’s the timer for?

A2 It tells you how much time has passed since you took the test saved in a particular slot last time.

Q3 Why IE is not supported?

A3 Why you still use IE?
