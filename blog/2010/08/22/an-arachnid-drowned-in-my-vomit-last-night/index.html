An arachnid drowned in my vomit last night

08-22-2010
music

An arachnid drowned in my vomit last night. Promise me you do not forget my efforts to maintain my affected simper in abhorrence.

Go diatonic if you wish to delve in music research in the way similar to natural language processing. Otherwise try hard to understand atonal like doing what you can to comprehend the first paragraph of this article. It DOES make sense to somebody who, jailed himself in a deserted place, never coming back.

Once you find Schoenberg makes sense, you are on the way to your prison’s construction. You carve the stones. You lay the bricks. You commence celebration on establishment of your penitentiary when you think the music meaningful. And you are alone. People refuse to discuss music with you because they don’t understand. People hate you when you explicate to them why it makes sense because they don’t want to hear. People laugh at you because they think you are bathetic and sanctimonious.

But you cannot escape. Your only opportunity of exodus is to put more people into your cell, until one day people can’t even notice which is outside and which is not. Go missionary go.
“These walls are funny. First you hate ‘em, then you get used to ‘em. Enough time passes, you get so you depend on them. That’s institutionalized.”
